Talk2PDF is a unique project that allows users to interact with PDF documents through a chat interface. Whether you need to extract information from resumes, study materials, or any other PDF document, Talk2PDF leverages advanced technologies to make the process intuitive and efficient.
![Talk2PDF](/talk2pdf.png)
## Usage

1. **Upload a PDF**: Click the "Upload PDF" button to select and upload a PDF document.
2. **Start Chatting**: Once the PDF is uploaded, you can start a conversation by typing your questions or requests in the chatbox.
3. **Interact with the PDF**: Chat naturally, and the system will respond based on the content of the PDF.

## Tech Stack

1. [NextJS](https://nextjs.org/) for frontend and backend
2. [LangChain](https://www.langchain.com/) to convert PDF documents into small chunks of text
3. [Open AI Embeddings API](https://platform.openai.com/docs/api-reference/embeddings/object) to generate the vector embeddings
4. [Pinecone DB](https://www.pinecone.io/) for storing vector embeddings
5. [Amazon S3](https://aws.amazon.com/s3/) to store the uploaded PDFs.
6. [Neon DB](https://neon.tech/): A serverless Postgres service** to store other user data.
7. [Clerk](https://clerk.com/) to manage user authentication
8. [Open AI Completions API](https://platform.openai.com/docs/api-reference/completions) to power the chat with the PDF
